About YBRS

Premium café energy, gallery-worthy detail.

We built YBRS to feel like a modern gallery you can taste—minimalist lines, bold art, and food that keeps you smiling.

Our Story

Artful spaces, crafted flavors.

YBRS began as a neighborhood café with walls that begged for art. We leaned into that instinct—curating paintings, plating food like small exhibits, and letting coffee aromas frame every visit.

  • Premium café that stays modern, warm, and minimal.
  • Food lineup grounded in pizzas, burgers, fries, coffee, and comfort combos.
  • Taglines that ring true: Taste brings smile on face. Burger for everyone.
  • Designed for conversations, co-working, and slow afternoons with a book.
